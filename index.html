ABOUT COLLEGE

Atma Ram Sanatan Dharma College, ranked 7th All India by the NIRF and accredited A++ by the NAAC with the highest score of 3.77, is a premier educational institution at the University of Delhi that aims to nurture excellence in education in a supportive and inclusive environment. ARSD College Campus

It emphasises fostering quality human resources and promoting productive ideas that benefit the students. Since its inception, the College has focused on character-building and idealism inspired by Sanatan values.

What Our Alumni Say

Our alumni are our pride. Hear from those who have been a part of ARSD College and are making a difference in their fields.

“

I started my theatre career from ARSD College in 2003 under the banner of Rangyan - The Dramatic Society of our college. Under the convenership of Dr. Gyantosh Kumar Jha, presently the Principal of ARSD College, I was given a chance to play a lead role in the play titled 'Ek Tha Gadha'. From there onwards, I worked hard and overcame each and every obstacle to achieve success in my life. I think that hard work is the key to success. Being given a chance, I think some of us would like to relive our college days and I happen to be one of those. I am proud to be an ARSDian.





Mr. Raj Kumar Rao
National Award Winning Actor
“

My association with this esteemed institution is a matter of immense pride and gratitude. The years I spent at ARSD College were instrumental in shaping my outlook towards life, strengthening my values, and inspiring me to serve society with dedication and integrity. The College’s commitment to quality education, research, innovation, cultural enrichment, sports, and community engagement reflects its vision of developing socially responsible, ethically grounded, and globally competent citizens. To the students, I would like to say that your years in college are a unique opportunity to discover your potential, cultivate knowledge, develop leadership qualities, and build lasting relationships. The future belongs to those who combine knowledge with character and ambition with humility.

Shri Ashish Sood
Hon’ble Minister
Government of NCT of Delhi
“

College days were filled with memories of fun- frolic, carefreeness, friends & long aimless discussions dotted with uncertainties of life. I was a science student who took up B.A. Political Science (Hons.), with no clue of the subject, but high on hopes as someone had guided me that this is the stepping stone to IAS. Thanks to our revered lecturers, each one was equally enthusiastic & eager to teach. I joined the Army after graduating. Life in the Army had been an adventurous journey. But it is indeed nostalgic to think of the wonderful college days at ARSDC. To conclude, I wish all the students a great inning in their college life.




Major General G. S. Talwar
Indian Armed Forces
“

According to my personal experiences, I believe that a school plays a very important role in guiding a student in the right direction in his life. After completing my school, I took admission in the North Campus of Delhi University but at that my primary concern was to look for the college which was closer to my home. Keeping that in my mind, I took admission in ARSD College. This proved to be a turning point in my life. Whatever I am today, whichever role I am playing today in the politics of India, the credit for shaping that personality of mine goes to the ARSD College and its eminent professors. The atmosphere that I got in ARSD College also worked in promoting the political capabilities within me. Today, I have got an opportunity to share my experiences in this special issue for which I convey my sincere thanks to the ARSD College and its editorial committee.
